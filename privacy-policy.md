# Genai News - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

## 1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy describes how Genai News ("we", "our", "us") handles information in connection with our automated news video publishing service. Genai News uses the TikTok Content Posting API to publish content to TikTok.

## 2. Information Collection
Genai News does not collect, store, or process any personal information from viewers or users. We do not require user accounts, logins, or registrations. Genai News operates as a content publisher only.

## 3. TikTok API Usage
Genai News uses the TikTok Content Posting API solely to publish original video content to our own TikTok account. We do not access, collect, or store any TikTok user data. We do not interact with other users' content or accounts through the API.

## 4. Third-Party Services
Genai News publishes content to the following platforms:
- TikTok (TikTok Privacy Policy applies: https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy)
- YouTube (Google Privacy Policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- Instagram (Meta Privacy Policy applies: https://privacycenter.instagram.com/policy)

Viewers' interactions with Genai News content on these platforms are governed by each platform's respective privacy policy.

## 5. Data Storage
Genai News does not maintain any databases of user information. All application data related to content generation is stored locally and is not shared with third parties.

## 6. Cookies and Tracking
Genai News does not use cookies, analytics, or any tracking technologies.

## 7. Children's Privacy
Genai News does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

## 8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
